Enter Your Private Key

Please enter your EOS private key. Your key is only used to sign a transaction locally and never leaves this application. Once your private key is entered, please verify your EOS account name.

Once you click Vote, your transaction will be delivered to the EOS blockchain and your vote will be registered.
